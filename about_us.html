Where We Began

Kenya Orient Insurance Ltd (KOIL) was incorporated in 1982 by the Al-Fateem Group of Dubai and was later acquired in 1988.

Since then, (1989), the company has recorded steady and impressive growth. KOIL is registered under the Insurance Act of Kenya and is authorized to under-write all classes of General Insurance Business.

In October 2004, the transfer of the firm to the new owners, was completed which led to changes in both the Board and the Management. These changes created a new vision for the company; which was focused on the idea of Delivering World Class Service to our clients, intermediaries and all other business associates within the greater East Africa Region.

The new leadership determined that Customer Service and New Product Development would be the key pillars for the company. KOIL has placed great importance on broker and agency relationships, informed by customer expectations.

The future is bright as the company’s growth will only be limited by the time it takes to hire knowledgeable associates committed to KOIL’s way of thinking, which can be summed up in these words: "Meeting all our customers’ insurance needs through great products and outstanding customer service.”

How We Conduct Our Business

Kenya Orient Insurance Limited integrates and manages social and environmental concerns in its business practice and operations while mutually interacting with its stakeholders.

It involves achieving success in ways that honor ethical values and respects people, communities and the natural environment.
